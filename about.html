About

Hi, I'm Callum.
I build websites
that actually work.

Swift7 is a one-person web design service based in Hull. I build professional, mobile-friendly websites for tradespeople and local service businesses across Yorkshire and the UK — at a price that makes sense for a small business.

The problem I kept seeing: decent businesses with no online presence, or outdated sites that were invisible on Google. Local plumbers, electricians, roofers, cleaners — people who are brilliant at what they do but losing jobs every day to competitors with better websites.

So I built Swift7 around one idea: get a proper business website live in 7 days for £250, all-in. No hidden fees. No ongoing monthly subscriptions. No watered-down template you have to write yourself. I write all the copy, set up your Google Business Profile, handle the domain and hosting, and hand over a site that's ready to take enquiries.

What I believe about web design for small businesses

  • →A website that ranks on Google is a sales tool that works 24 hours a day. It's the highest ROI thing most small businesses can invest in.
  • →Speed matters more than complexity. A fast, clear, mobile-friendly site will always outperform an over-engineered one that takes months to build.
  • →You should own your website outright. No platform lock-in, no disappearing site if you miss a payment.
  • →Content written by someone who understands your trade converts better than a generic template you've had to fill in yourself.
  • →7 days is enough time to do it properly — if the process is tight and you've done it before.

The work

Every Swift7 site includes up to 10 pages, written content throughout, on-page SEO, Google Business Profile setup, domain, hosting, SSL, and a mobile-first design. You get a preview before launch, and the site goes live when you're happy with it — within 7 days, guaranteed.
