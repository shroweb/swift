Day 1 — Your brief

What we need from you

The brief takes around 10 minutes. We'll ask you a few questions to understand your business and your customers. That's it.

  • A short description of what you do and who you serve
  • Your logo (if you have one — we can work without it)
  • Any existing images you want to use
  • Your preferred contact method for customers
  • Your domain if you already have one
Days 2–5 — We build

What we do while you wait

We write every word on every page, design a clean mobile-first layout, and set up all the technical essentials. You don't need to do a thing.

  • Up to 10 pages written and designed from scratch
  • 5 SEO articles targeting your local search terms
  • Mobile-first design, fast-loading and cross-browser tested
  • SEO foundations, meta tags, and site structure
  • Enquiry forms wired to your inbox
  • Domain, hosting, SSL, and email addresses configured
Day 6 — Review

One round of revisions

You get a private preview link and a chance to flag anything. We handle sensible changes same day so the schedule stays on track.

  • View the full site on a private preview URL
  • Request text changes, layout tweaks, or corrections
  • We turn around revisions quickly — same day where possible
  • No back-and-forth meetings needed
Day 7 — Go live

Everything handed over

We publish your site, submit it to Google, and set up your Business Profile. From day one, you're visible and ready to take enquiries.

  • Site published to your live domain
  • Submitted to Google Search Console for indexing
  • Google Business Profile set up and optimised
  • Google Analytics installed and tracking
  • Login details and instructions sent to you
