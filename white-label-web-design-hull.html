FAQ

Common questions
from partners.

Will my client know you built the site? +
No. The delivered site has zero Swift7 references — no links, no credits, no comments in the code. We can also sign an NDA before the project starts to give you full legal protection.
Can I handle the domain and hosting myself? +
Yes. If you want to control the hosting relationship with your client, we can hand over clean deployable files that you put live yourself. Or we manage it all and you take the credit.
What if my client wants changes after launch? +
We can handle ongoing edits at an hourly rate (billed to you, confidentially). Or if you'd prefer, we hand over full access so you can make changes yourself. Either way, your client always deals with you.
Do you do volume discounts for multiple sites? +
Not currently — the £250 price is already very lean. If you're sending consistent volume, get in touch and we can talk through what works. We'd rather build a good long-term relationship than haggle per project.
How do we communicate during the project? +
Email or WhatsApp — whichever works for you. You'll deal directly with Callum throughout. No project management tools, no ticketing systems, no handoffs.
